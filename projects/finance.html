Finance Management App

UI/UX project for expense tracking and budget planning.

  • Designed in Figma & Adobe XD
  • 10+ wireframes
  • User research with 20+ users
  • Responsive and accessible design

Designed a finance management application to help users track expenses, set budgets, and analyze financial data, improving financial planning by 30%. Created 10+ wireframes and interactive prototypes using Figma/Adobe XD, ensuring a 50% faster design-to-development process. Focused on responsive design and accessibility, making the app 100% mobile-friendly. Conducted user research with 20+ participants, leading to a 40% improvement in user engagement. Implemented a modern UI with consistent typography, color schemes, and intuitive navigation, enhancing usability by 35%
